== Installation ==

1. Wordpress -> Plugins,  點擊 “Add New”.  在搜索框內搜索“ScanForPay”.
2. 點擊Install, 然後點擊activate來激活插件.
3. 聯絡[ScanForPay](https://hk.scanforpay.com), 申請您的ScanForPay商戶帳號.
4. 申請成功後, 您會獲得您的商戶號, partnerNo和signKey, 填入ScanForPay的插件設置裡
5. 您可以開始支付寶、微信收款啦!

1. Wordpress -> Plugins,  click “Add New”.  search ScanForPay”.
2. Click Install, and then click activate.
3. Contact[ScanForPay](https://hk.scanforpay.com) to apply for an account.
4. Once apply successfully, you will get your merchant No., partnerNo and signKey, fill in the plugin configurations.
5. Start using now!


== FAQ ==
* 我為什麼需要開通支付寶/AlipayHK/WechatPay收款?

現在支付寶的全球用戶已經超過10億, WechatPay用戶數已經超過9億，中國的電商用戶超過8億, 電商海淘用戶也已超過1億. 滿足中國用戶的支付需要和習慣已經成為很多商戶的剛需.

並且支付寶、微信目前專門成立了AlipayHK、WechatPayHK針對香港本地用戶進行推廣, 有更多的優惠和方便功能推出.

* ScanForPay是免费的么?

ScanForPay的WooCommerce插件是完全免費的, 開通香港的商戶版支付寶、微信目前也完全免費, 但是支付寶、微信會對每筆交易收取一定的手續費. 具體手續費用請聯絡並諮詢我們.

網站: [https://hk.scanforpay.com](https://hk.scanforpay.com)
電話: (852)-81207808

* 我可以使用個人支付寶、微信進行收款麼? 

不可以, 個人支付寶、微信只能在支付寶、微信內轉帳, 並沒有提供開放的接口在網店中使用.

更多問題, 請訪問我們的[常見問題](https://hk.scanforpay.com/help/index.html)


* Why you should accept Alipay/AlipayHK/WechatPay in your store?
The global users of Alipay has been over 1 billion now, and the users of WechatPay has also been more than 900 million. And there're 800 million e-commerce users, in which 100 million users have experience with shopping from overseas. So it's an urgent needs for merchants to provide a convenient payment method for Chinese customers.

And also, there will be many promotions and convenient functions for HongKong local users by using AliapyHK and WechatPayHK.

* Is it free to use ScanForPay?

It's totally free to use our WooCommerce plugin, and opening account is also a service for free.
But Alipay, WechatPay would charge for each transaction. For more details please contact us.

website: [https://hk.scanforpay.com](https://hk.scanforpay.com)
tel: (852)-81207808

* Can I use my personal Alipay, WechatPay to collect money?

No, you can't. You can only tranfer your money to others in Alipay/Wechat by using the personal version. There's no open APIs can be used in an online shop.
